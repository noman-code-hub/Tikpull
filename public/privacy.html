No Personal Accounts

TikPull does not require a user account. We do not ask for your password, TikTok login, or private profile data. We do not sell personal data that you give through an account system.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have privacy rights. These may include access, correction, deletion, objection, or data portability rights. You can also limit cookies through your browser. Contact us if you need help with a privacy request.

Contact Information

For privacy questions, use our contact form at /contact. Please include enough detail for us to understand your request. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
