About TikPull

TikPull is a simple online tool for saving public TikTok videos offline. It is made for students, creators, editors, marketers, and everyday users. Many people need quick access to clips they already found on TikTok. You can use it from a phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop browser. The goal is to make downloading easier. There are no confusing steps, paid accounts, or extra software.

The tool works in a simple way. You copy a public TikTok video link and paste it into TikPull. Our server reads the public video page and looks for available video or audio files. When TikTok provides a supported source, TikPull shows download choices. You can save video as MP4 or audio when available. The process is designed to feel quick and clear.

We want TikPull to stay free, useful, and privacy-friendly. The service does not ask for an account. We do not store downloaded videos on our server. We only process links long enough to return available download choices. Users should respect creators, privacy, and copyright. TikPull is built for personal, fair, and responsible use of public content.

Disclaimer: We do not host any videos. All videos belong to their original creators on TikTok.
